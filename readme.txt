With Mosmos, your customers can book any product and pay the balance in flexible installments at 0% interest.

== Description ==

The plugin adds a button on the WooCommerce product page so your customers can book any product and pay the balance in flexible installments at 0% interest. You will receive and manage all orders on your Mosmos Business dashboard.

= Why Mosmos Direct? =

* Increase your sales by accepting flexible payments from your customers

== Installation ==

1. Download and activate the plugin
2. Navigate to `Settings > Mosmos` to configure
3. Update Settings based on requested inputs and save
